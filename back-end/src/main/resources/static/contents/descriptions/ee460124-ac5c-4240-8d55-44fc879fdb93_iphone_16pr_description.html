iPhone 16 Pro: Power Meets Precision

Introducing the iPhone 16 Pro, a masterpiece of technology and design. Featuring a stunning 6.1-inch Super Retina XDR display with ProMotion technology, the iPhone 16 Pro delivers an ultra-smooth experience with an adaptive refresh rate of up to 120Hz. Every scroll, game, and video feels incredibly responsive and fluid.

Under the hood, the iPhone 16 Pro is powered by the next-generation A17 Bionic chip, providing extraordinary speed and efficiency. With advanced CPU and GPU performance, you can seamlessly multitask, play graphically intense games, and run the latest apps without a hitch.

Triple-Camera System

Capture your world like never before with the advanced triple-camera system. Featuring a 48 MP main camera, ultra-wide lens, and telephoto lens, the iPhone 16 Pro delivers professional-quality photos and videos. With Night mode, Smart HDR 5, and ProRAW capabilities, your shots will be stunning in any lighting condition. Enjoy cinematic video recording in Dolby Vision and take advantage of improved optical zoom for unmatched versatility.

Stay connected with the power of 5G technology, enabling faster downloads, smoother streaming, and a more responsive online experience. All-Day Battery Life and MagSafe

Experience all-day battery life, keeping you powered throughout your busy day. With MagSafe technology, wireless charging is faster and more convenient, and a range of magnetic accessories open up new possibilities for your device.

Running on iOS 17, the iPhone 16 Pro provides an enhanced user experience with new features, advanced privacy controls, and greater customization options. From productivity to entertainment, the iPhone 16 Pro is designed to elevate your digital lifestyle.
