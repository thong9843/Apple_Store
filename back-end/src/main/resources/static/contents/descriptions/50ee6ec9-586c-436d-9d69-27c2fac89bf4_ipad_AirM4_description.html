iPad Air M4: Powerful, Colorful, and Versatile

Meet the new iPad Air M4, packed with power and elegance to elevate your creativity, productivity, and entertainment. With the advanced Apple M4 chip, this iPad Air delivers lightning-fast performance and outstanding graphics, making it perfect for multitasking, editing high-resolution videos, or playing graphically demanding games.

Stunning 10.9-inch Liquid Retina Display

The 10.9-inch Liquid Retina display offers a vibrant, edge-to-edge viewing experience with True Tone, P3 wide color, and anti-reflective coating. Whether you're drawing, watching your favorite series, or editing photos, everything looks brilliant and lifelike on the iPad Air M4.

Ultra-Portable and Stylish Design

The iPad Air M4 is designed to be ultra-thin and lightweight, making it incredibly easy to take anywhere. With a sleek aluminum body and an array of gorgeous color options, it's as stylish as it is functional. Perfect for students, professionals, and creatives on the go.

Advanced Camera System

Take stunning photos and crisp 4K videos with the 12 MP wide rear camera or enjoy smooth, high-quality video calls and selfies with the 12 MP ultra-wide front camera with Center Stage. The Center Stage feature automatically keeps you in the frame, making video chats more engaging.

All-Day Battery Life and USB-C

With all-day battery life, the iPad Air M4 is ready to work and play whenever you are. The USB-C port allows for faster charging and easy connectivity to a wide range of accessories, making your workflow even more efficient.

Compatible with Apple Pencil and Magic Keyboard

Unleash your creativity with the Apple Pencil (2nd generation), perfect for drawing, note-taking, or marking up documents. Pair the iPad Air M4 with the Magic Keyboard for a comfortable typing experience and laptop-like productivity.

Running on iPadOS, the iPad Air M4 offers a seamless and intuitive user experience, with features that help you get more done and enjoy your favorite apps like never before. The iPad Air M4: where power meets portability, wrapped in a beautiful, versatile design.
