14-inch Liquid Retina XDR Display

Immerse yourself in the stunning 14-inch Liquid Retina XDR display, offering up to 1000 nits of sustained brightness and a 1,000,000:1 contrast ratio. The M1 Pro chip delivers up to 10-core CPU and up to 16-core GPU performance, ensuring lightning-fast execution of complex workflows. The 16-core Neural Engine enhances machine learning tasks, making the MacBook Pro M1 14-inch a powerhouse for professionals who demand speed and efficiency.

All-Day Battery Life

With up to 17 hours of battery life, the MacBook Pro M1 keeps you productive all day. Whether you’re working on location or from your favorite café, you can stay focused without worrying about charging.

Advanced Connectivity and Design

The MacBook Pro’s sleek aluminum design is both elegant and robust, perfect for professionals on the move.

macOS Monterey

Running on macOS Monterey, the MacBook Pro M1 offers powerful productivity features, seamless integration with other Apple devices, and advanced privacy protections. Take advantage of features like Focus mode, enhanced FaceTime, and more to optimize your workflow.
